## Suggested progression

**Phase 1: Python Fundamentals (Syntax to OOP)**
1. Complete sections 01 to 03 first.
2. Move to 04 and 05 with small coding exercises.
3. Finish 06 and 07 carefully, then revise by solving section 09.
4. Use 08 only after basics are clear.

**Phase 2: AI/ML Foundations (NumPy to Scikit-Learn)**
5. Section 10: NumPy for numerical computing (2-3 hours)
6. Section 11: Pandas for data manipulation (3-4 hours)
7. Section 12: Matplotlib/Seaborn for visualization (2-3 hours)
8. Section 13: Math foundations for ML (3-4 hours)
9. Section 14: Scikit-Learn algorithms (4-5 hours)
10. Section 15: Complete ML workflows and best practices (2-3 hours)
